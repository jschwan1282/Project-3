Visualizing Malaria's Effect on the World

In this project, we utilized the Javascript library Leaflet to create an interactive map that shows the relationship between deaths caused by Malaria and GDP per capita. Our Leaflet maps' base layer shows GDP by color scale. Upon clicking each country, statistics will display. There are also color-coded circles that indicate Malaria deaths in each country. In our second and third visualizations, we used the Javascript library Charts to help display the global trend in Malaria deaths over the 2000s and further display GDP per capita vs. Malaria deaths. Click 'Maps & Charts' in our navigation or the images below to check out our data visualizations. Our project is backed by a Python script that feeds a CSV document (see data source above in navigation) into a PostgreSQL database and table.

---

According to the World Health Organization, there were approximately 219 million Malaria cases worldwide in 2017.
